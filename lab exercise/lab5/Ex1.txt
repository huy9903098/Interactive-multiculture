4. What are CDNs? What kind of benefits you get if you use them?
- CDNs are servers that delivery web content and pages based on geographical location of the user.
- The benefits of CDNs:
  . Better performance: when the end user want to get the content, they can get it from the nearest CDN server rather than go through the original.
  . Availability: when there are excessive user traffic, instead of everything is absorbed by the content provider, it is
distributed over to many server  infrastructure
  - Increase in security.
